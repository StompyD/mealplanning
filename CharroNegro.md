# Charro Negro

## Ingredients
- 2 ounces [tequila repasado](./TequilaCocktails.md)
- 1/2 lime
- 4 ounces Mexican Coke (made with cane sugar)

Combine rum, juice form half lime, and coke in a glass over ice. Keep lime cup in glass.
