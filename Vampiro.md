# Vampiro

## Ingredients
- 1.5 ounces of [blanco tequila](./TequilaCocktails.md)
- 3 ounces sangrita (Viuda de Sanchez)
- 1.5 ounces citrus soda (Squirt)
- 1 ounce lime juice
- Pinch of Tajin 

## Directions
Rim half of glass with Tajin. Mix ingredients with ice in glass. Garnish with lime wedge or jalapeno.
