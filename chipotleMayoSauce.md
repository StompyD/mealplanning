# jose.elcook's Chipotle Mayo Sauce

[Recipe Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/xGW1uGFfVNI)

## Ingredients
- 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo
- 1 Tbsp Honey
- Juice of 1 lime
- 2 oz pineapple
- 1 oz cilantro
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black peppers
- 1 tsp garlic (2-4 cloves)
- 4 oz (1/2 cup) mayo

## Instructions
1. Blend everything except the mayo on high speed until smooth
2. Add mayo and combine on low speed.
